The Fraudulent Network

A network of 14 suppliers (12 new, 2 hijacked) operated in a coordinated rotation pattern across 6 states, vanishing after 2 months.

Investigation Hurdles

The Jurisdictional Maze and time lags of manual investigation made the overarching pattern invisible to individual state officers.

8+ Months

Estimated manual investigation time.

The Solution: Advanced Analytics

1. Pattern Analysis

Compares current filings against a multi-year historical baseline to instantly flag the high-velocity supplier rotation as a statistically significant anomaly.

2. Link Analysis

Goes beyond transactions to correlate non-financial data, uncovering hidden connections like common IP addresses, intermediaries, and bank details across states.

3. Financial Viability

Calculates the ITC Sufficiency Ratio. A ratio below 5% proves the entities have no legitimate business activity and exist only to pass fraudulent credit.

4. Intelligence Dossier

Consolidates all findings into a single, actionable report, providing the IO with a ready-to-act package for swift, targeted enforcement.
