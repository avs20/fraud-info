The Turning Point: AI Uncovers the Truth

While manual checks found no issues, an AI-powered system analyzed multiple datasets simultaneously. It saw through the facade, flagging deep, correlated anomalies invisible to the human eye. The system identified five critical red flags.

Red Flag 1: Coordinated Registration

The AI detected that the "independent" suppliers were likely established in a coordinated effort. All five registered within an unusually narrow 45-day window, a statistical improbability for genuinely separate businesses.

Red Flag 2: Illicit Financial Flows

Analysis of fund flows revealed a circular pattern designed to siphon money back. For every payment made, nearly all of it was immediately transferred out, leaving an impossible 2-3% margin for the suppliers.

Red Flag 3: Business Capacity Mismatch

The AI cross-referenced GST data with real-world economic indicators. Suppliers claiming massive turnovers had a physical footprint that didn't match, such as negligible electricity usage and zero registered employees.

Red Flag 4: Identical Invoice Patterns

Digital analysis of invoices showed they were not from independent sources. Invoice numbers across all five "different" companies followed a sequential pattern, and digital fingerprints proved they were generated from the same source.

Red Flag 5: Coordinated Network Activity

The entire network's transaction activity was synchronized. All five suppliers showed sharp peaks in activity only at the end of financial quarters—precisely when Rajesh Steel needed to maximize its ITC claims—before going completely dormant.

The Resolution: Dismantling the Network

Armed with the AI's precise intelligence, investigators launched a targeted operation. The findings were quickly verified, leading to decisive action that dismantled the entire fraudulent network.
